How to Install (Sideload)

Prerequisites (Do This First)

You must enable developer options on your watch before you can use either method below.

Step 1: Enable Developer Options on Your Watch

  1. On your watch, go to Settings.
  2. Scroll down and tap on System → About.
  3. Find Build number.
  4. Tap it 7 times until you see “You are now a developer!”.

Step 2: Enable ADB & Wireless Debugging

  1. Go to Developer options in Settings.
  2. Enable ADB debugging and Wireless debugging.
  3. Note the IP:Port displayed (e.g., 192.168.1.100:5555).

Choose Your Installation Method

Step 3: Sideload from Phone

  1. Install Bugjaeger or Wear Installer 2 on your phone.
  2. Ensure phone and watch are on the same Wi-Fi.
  3. Open Bugjaeger → tap the “Connect” icon → enter your watch’s IP:Port.
  4. When prompted, allow debugging on your watch.
  5. Tap “Packages” → “+” → choose the downloaded APK → Install.
  6. Wait for “Success”.

Step 3: Sideload from PC (using adb)

  1. Download Android Platform Tools.
  2. Unzip to a folder (e.g., C:\platform-tools).
  3. Open terminal in that folder:
    • Windows: Shift + Right-click → “Open PowerShell here”.
    • Mac/Linux: Open Terminal → cd ~/platform-tools.
  4. Ensure same Wi-Fi connection as your watch.
  5. adb connect 192.168.1.100:5555
  6. Allow debugging on your watch.
  7. adb install "C:\Users\You\Downloads\com.merciless.sundowning.apk"

Final Step

Step 4: Disable ADB

After installing, turn off ADB debugging and Wireless debugging to save battery.
